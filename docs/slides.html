ghcr.io/theskygold/track2-captioner
The problem

Two scores that fight each other

Track 2 grades every caption on accuracy (does it match the video?) and style match (does the tone land?) — across four tones of the same clip. Push for vivid tone and you drift from the facts. Play it safe and you say nothing. You have to win both.

The idea

Three models see more. Agreement lies less.

frames→ GPT‑5.5Gemini 3.1 ProOpus 4.5 →cross‑reference + write→ 4 styled captions

Each model lists everything it sees, independently. A writer fuses them: a detail two or more models agree on is trusted; a lone, unverifiable guess is dropped. Detection = the union of what they see. Precision = their agreement.

We built an adversarial vision audit — agents that read the real frames and try to refute every claim — and retired the unreliable proxy judge. The ensemble even reads a street sign no single model gets right, by agreement.

Not a demo — a product

Runs on any video, deploys anywhere

  • Upload or paste a URL → get all four styles (web app).
  • Reproducible Docker image: reads /input, writes /output, linux/amd64, ≈1 GB, no keys in the repo.
  • Graceful degradation, multi‑provider failover, filter + safety guards, guarded by CI.
Use of AMD platforms
